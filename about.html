So who is Erik Martus?

In short, he is me.

The long answer: I am a web and graphic designer based out of Philadelphia, PA. I am currently a second year student at Drexel University's Antoinette Westphal College of Media Arts and Design majoring in Interactive Digital Media (IDM for short).

IDM is a vague name for an exciting degree covering web design and development, user interface design and user experience design with a sprinkle of mobile application development. For me this isn't quite enough so I am double minoring in psychology to really delve into how humans tick, and graphic design to follow my passion for print design and typography.
